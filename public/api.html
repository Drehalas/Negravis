🚀 Negravis Oracle API

Interact with our decentralized oracle network powered by 0G.AI and Fluence Network. Test AI services, manage accounts, and explore TEE-verified computations.

GET
/api/account/balance
Retrieve your current account balance and ledger information from the 0G Compute Network.
POST
/api/account/deposit
Deposit funds to your ledger account for AI service payments and network fees.
GET
/api/services/list
List all available AI services including Llama-3.3-70B and DeepSeek-R1-70B models.
POST
/api/services/chat
Send chat requests to AI models with TEE verification and automatic payment processing.
GET
/api/services/providers
Get information about available service providers and their acknowledgment status.
